Music/Songs

Music, one of the most influencial forms of art that the world has ever been able to experience.

It has the ability to make people feel different emotions and even tell stories from the artists themselves.

It is one of the best thingss because it give you a feeling of connectivity with the artists and bands that created these pieces.

Now, here are some of the songs that have been some of the best one that I have listened to and their respective artists/bands

Black Thunder by The HU feat. Serjo Tankian & DL of Bad Wolves

This has been on of the most recent songs that I have started listening to as of the last couple of weeks.

When I first listened to it, it was as if I was listening to a tale of a great battle from long ago. One where many were lost and legends were created.

It also felt as if I could hear the passion and cries of the warriors through the way the artists performed and how the vocalists sang this song.

Did You See the Sunrise? by Chogakusei

I Was working on assignments while I had Youtube on autoplay and when I heard it, it was a mix of emotions.

It felt as if I were hearing of a hero's motivational speech or a promise that brings about an inspiration or strong emotion into the listener.

It really is an amazing song alongside how Chogakusei main lead vocalist gave his all to this song.

Plan Z by BOI WHAT

I have only recently heard this song and it is something that needs to be truly experienced.

While this song has the AI-generated voice of Plankton from Spongebob Squarepants, there was still a person that wrote, sang, and produced this beauty that it feels as if you are hearing Plankton's plan for getting the secret formula.

Neon Tide by BOI WHAT

This is also another song that I have heard that is still voiced by AI but is still so incredible to listen to!

It is a sequel to Plan Z and this one follows up amazingly well when it comes to the lyrics, intensity, and incredible action from Boi What
