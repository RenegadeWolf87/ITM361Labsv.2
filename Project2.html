Book Series Review for "A Song of Ice and Fire

Today, we will be doing a quick review of the book series that came from the mind of the great George R. R. Martin. It is also still continuing on with two more volumes that will be coming to shelves in the near future to complete this 7 volume series. This series in question being "A Song of Ice and Fire" and its volumes "A Game of Thrones", "A Clash of Kings", "A Storm of Swords", "A Feast of Crows", and "A Dance with Dragons". We will be giving a honest opinion to each of these books and will make sure to give as much of our opinion as possible! Now, let's begin!
